About Us

Founded by a Charleston local with a passion for environmental sustainability and clean neighborhoods, Lowcountry Bin Cleaners was born to meet a simple but overlooked need — clean, odor-free trash bins. We use a self-contained mobile unit that captures dirty water for safe disposal, and we never use harsh chemicals that harm the environment.

Our mission is to provide affordable, eco-conscious services to every corner of the Lowcountry. Whether you're a busy family, a vacation rental host, or a local business, we’re here to help make your curb appeal shine.
